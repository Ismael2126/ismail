Building Skills for Tomorrow’s Digital World

Coding with Purpose, Led by Adam Waheed

We are learning to code under the guidance of Adam Waheed, who has been an incredible mentor throughout our journey.

His teaching style is clear, engaging, and truly inspiring. Adam goes beyond just explaining concepts—he empowers us to think logically, solve problems creatively, and build confidence in coding.

He has professional experience working on the Bandharu software at Maldives Ports Limited, where he contributed to improving port management systems.

In 2020, Adam and his friends developed a powerful and user-friendly HR software that streamlined employee and administrative operations for many businesses.

We deeply appreciate the time, effort, and passion he invests in helping us grow. Thanks to his dedication, a whole new generation is being shaped with the skills and mindset needed to thrive in the digital world. ---Written by Ismail Ahmed
